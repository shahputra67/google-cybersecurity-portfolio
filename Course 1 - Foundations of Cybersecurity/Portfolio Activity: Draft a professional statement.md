# Professional Statement Activity

## Activity Overview
This activity focuses on writing a professional statement to include in a cybersecurity portfolio.  
A professional statement introduces who you are, your values, and your interest in cybersecurity.  
It helps employers understand your strengths and the value you can bring to their organization.  

You will draft a short statement that highlights your:
- Strengths and values  
- Interest in cybersecurity  
- How your skills support an organization’s security goals  

You should update this statement as your knowledge and experience grow.

---

## Scenario
You are preparing to enter the cybersecurity field and want a statement ready for job applications.  
The goal is to create a short, clear introduction for employers that reflects your personality and goals.

---

## Steps
1. Review your outline from the professional statement template.  
2. Identify your strengths, values, and reasons for entering cybersecurity.  
3. Write 2–3 sentences describing how those traits help organizations improve security.  
4. Use examples like:  
   - *I am a detail-oriented cybersecurity analyst who works to identify and reduce system vulnerabilities to protect data integrity.*  
   - *I value teamwork and continuous learning to strengthen an organization’s overall security posture.*  
5. Save your statement and include it in your cybersecurity portfolio.  
6. Revisit and refine it as you gain more experience.

---

## Self-Assessment (5 Points)
Review your work and answer “yes” or “no” for each point:

1. I described how I want to be perceived by employers.  
2. I included my strengths and values.  
3. I explained my interest in cybersecurity.  
4. I connected my abilities to organizational security goals.  
5. I plan to update my statement as I gain more knowledge.  

Aim for at least 4 out of 5 points before finalizing.
